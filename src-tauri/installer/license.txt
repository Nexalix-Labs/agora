Basic policy

- The app installs per-user and stores its settings locally on your machine
  (%APPDATA%\com.nexalix.agora). Nothing is uploaded.
- No telemetry, no analytics, no accounts.
- Network access: update checks against github.com/Nexalix-Labs/agora
  (signed releases), and optional plugins you enable (e.g. crypto rates via
  CoinGecko, web search). Disable them in Settings -> Plugins at any time.
